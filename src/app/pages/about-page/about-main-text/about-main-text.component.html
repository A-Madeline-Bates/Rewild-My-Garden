Rewild My Garden helps you to bring the rewilding movement into your back garden, regardless of how big or small it may be. Fill out the questions on the Make My Garden Wild page to access personalised advice about how to how to make your outdoor space a welcoming home for hedgehogs, birds, pollinators, and amphibians.

We hope that our advice will help you to create a space which is wonderful for both you and nature.

Rewild My Garden would like to offer thanks to everyone who has contributed their time to help with the project, with special thanks to Plants for a Future, BS3 Wildlife Group and Nicholas Tew.
