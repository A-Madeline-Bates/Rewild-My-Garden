About Rewild My Garden!

Why Rewilding?

The aim of the project is to build a website which encourages people to manage small areas of private land for wildlife. Although there is currently a large degree of public interest in rewilding, many people lack the resources to know how to rewild an area effectively, or only have access to a very small quantity of land on which their efforts might not feel meaningful.
